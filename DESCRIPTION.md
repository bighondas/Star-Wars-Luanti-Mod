# Star Wars Mod for Minetest

Bring the Star Wars universe to Minetest!

Choose your faction with:

`/team jedi` or `/team sith`

## Jedi Abilities

* **Dash:** Sneak + Up
* **Force Jump:** Sneak + Jump
* **Force Push:** Sneak + Use (while looking at a player)
* **Saber Throw:** Sneak + Use (while holding an activated lightsaber)
* **Heal**
* **Force Sense**
* **Crystal Bond:** Use while holding a blank Kyber Crystal

## Sith Abilities

* **Dash:** Sneak + Up
* **Force Jump:** Sneak + Jump
* **Force Pull:** Sneak + Use (while looking at a player)
* **Saber Throw:** Sneak + Use (while holding an activated lightsaber)
* **Force Choke:** Sneak + Use (while looking at a player)
* **Force Lightning:** Sneak + Use (while looking at a player)
* **Crystal Bleed:** Use while holding a blank Kyber Crystal

Switch between your available Force abilities using the **Aux1** key (default **E**). Your selected ability and current faction are displayed in the bottom-left corner of the screen.

## Jedi Ranks

* Force Sensitive
* Youngling
* Padawan
* Knight
* Master
* Grand Master

## Sith Ranks

* Force Sensitive
* Servant
* Warrior
* Killer
* Lord
* Dark Lord

Advance through the ranks by completing quests from **Yoda** (Jedi) or **Darth Sidious** (Sith). Each new rank unlocks additional Force abilities.

## Features

* Craftable lightsabers:

  * Single
  * Shoto
  * Double-Bladed
  * Curved-Hilt
  * Crossguard
* Craftable blasters
* Beskar ore, found deep underground
* Vehicles:

  * X-Wing
  * TIE Fighter
  * Speeder
* Vehicles can only be dismantled using the **Wrench** tool.
* Fire blasters with the **Aux1** key (default **E**).

May the Force be with you!
